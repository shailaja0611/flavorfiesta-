Cooking Guides and Their Benefits

What are Cooking Guides?


Cooking guides are comprehensive resources designed to help individuals improve their cooking skills. They cover various aspects of cooking, from basic techniques to advanced recipes, and often include tips on ingredient selection, kitchen tools, and meal planning.

Benefits of Cooking Guides


  • Improved Skills: Learn new techniques and recipes.
  • Healthier Meals: Guidance on nutritious cooking.
  • Time-Saving: Efficient meal prep and cooking methods.
  • Cost-Effective: Tips on budget-friendly cooking.
  • Culinary Exploration: Exposure to different cuisines.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

  • 1 cup Chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 2 tablespoons of Oil
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 teaspoon of Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of Chole Masala

Information:

  • Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Then, add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  • Mix in the red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Stir well.
  • Add the paneer cubes and mix gently. Pour in the heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Season with salt and garnish with coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
